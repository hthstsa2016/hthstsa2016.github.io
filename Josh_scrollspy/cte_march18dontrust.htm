CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION


U.S. News & World Report named High Technology High School the #1 STEM school in the entire nation. Specializing in science, mathematics, engineering, and technology, High Tech offers a multitude of Project Lead The Way courses that will fully prepare its students for a bright future in any one of these specific fields. From freshman to senior, the curriculum always remains challenging, yet rewarding at the same time.

A FRESH START


When you first walk into High Technology High School as an incoming freshman, you are immediately immersed in a purely engineering environment. With two unique engineering classes, being "Introduction to Engineering Design" and "Software Applications", students are given a myriad of opportunities to apply themselves in this field and take off on the journey of finding their inner engineer.

TAKING IT A LEVEL HIGHER


Sophomore year at High Technology High School allows each student to enhance their knowledge of the engineering field, with two more insightful courses: "Principles of Engineering" and "Computer Integrated Manufacturing." Each course delves into the attributes of mechanincal engineering, programming, and real-world applications of the Project Lead The Way design process.

CHOOSING THE RIGHT PATH


Choosing the right field in engineering is always a hard decision. However, junior year at High Technology High School offers 4 specific technology electives that will fulfill the desire to excel in a specific field or simply help each student realize their place in the vast world of STEM. Consisting of "Computer Science and Software Engineering", "Digital Electronics", "Civil Engineering and Architecture", and "Biological Engineering and Ecological Sustainability", our students are completely able to map out their futures.
